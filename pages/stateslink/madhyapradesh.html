Tourists places in MadhyaPradesh

1. Khajuraho

Location: Chhatarpur district, Madhya Pradesh

Famous For: UNESCO World Heritage Site, famous for intricate erotic temple carvings

  • Attractions: Western Group of Temples, Kandariya Mahadev Temple, Lakshmana Temple
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Dal Bafla, Poha, Chaat
  • Hotels & Stay: Radisson Jass Hotel, The Lalit Temple View
  • Restaurants: Open Air Restaurant, Green View Restaurant
  • Why Famous: Stunning medieval temples with detailed sculptures depicting ancient Indian art and culture

2. Bhopal

Location: Central Madhya Pradesh

Famous For: Lakes, historic mosques, and museums

  • Attractions: Upper Lake, Taj-ul-Masajid, Bharat Bhavan, Van Vihar National Park
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Bhopali Gosht Korma, Poha, Jalebi
  • Hotels & Stay: Jehan Numa Palace, Noor-Us-Sabah Palace
  • Restaurants: Manohar Dairy, Black Tusk
  • Why Famous: Blend of rich history and natural beauty with lakes and gardens

  • Hotels & Stay: Hotel Maharaja, The Mystic Ujjain
  • Restaurants: Mangal Bhavan, Dewali Restaurant
  • Why Famous: Religious importance and spiritual aura with historic temples
