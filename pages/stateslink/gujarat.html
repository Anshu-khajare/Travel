Tourists places in Gujarat

1. Ahmedabad

Location: Central Gujarat

Famous For: Sabarmati Ashram, cultural heritage, textiles

  • Attractions: Sabarmati Ashram, Bhadra Fort, Kankaria Lake, Adalaj Stepwell
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Dhokla, Fafda, Jalebi
  • Hotels & Stay: Hyatt Ahmedabad, The House of MG
  • Restaurants: Agashiye, Rajwadu
  • Why Famous: UNESCO World Heritage City known for Gandhi’s legacy and architecture

2. White Desert of Kutch

Location: Great Rann of Kutch, western Gujarat

Famous For: Rann Utsav, salt desert landscape

  • Attractions: Rann Utsav, White Rann, Kalo Dungar
  • Best Time to Visit: November to February
  • Famous Food: Kutchi Dabeli, Bajra Rotla
  • Hotels & Stay: Tent City Dhordo, Rann Resort
  • Restaurants: Local food stalls at festival site
  • Why Famous: Unique white salt desert, cultural festival and folk performances

3. Gir National Park

Location: Junagadh district, Southern Gujarat

Famous For: Asiatic lions

  • Attractions: Lion Safari, Devalia Safari Park
  • Best Time to Visit: December to March (Park closed mid-June to mid-October)
  • Famous Food: Kathiyawadi thali
  • Hotels & Stay: Gir Jungle Lodge, Fern Gir Forest Resort
  • Restaurants: Resorts and local dhabas
  • Why Famous: The only place to see Asiatic lions in the wild

4. Somnath

Location: Prabhas Patan, near Veraval

Famous For: Somnath Jyotirlinga Temple

  • Attractions: Somnath Temple, Triveni Sangam, Light and Sound Show
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Gujarati thali, local sweets
  • Hotels & Stay: Lords Inn, Sagar Darshan
  • Restaurants: Shivam Restaurant, Bharat Hotel
  • Why Famous: One of the 12 Jyotirlinga shrines of Lord Shiva

5. Statue of Unity

Location: Kevadia, Narmada district

Famous For: World's tallest statue (of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel)

  • Attractions: Viewing gallery, Valley of Flowers, Sardar Sarovar Dam, Light and Sound Show
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Local Gujarati cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Tent City Narmada, Ramada Encore
  • Restaurants: Unity Food Court
  • Why Famous: Tallest statue in the world, symbol of national unity

6. Dwarkadhish Temple

Location: Dwarka, Gujarat

Famous For: Sacred Hindu pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Krishna

  • Attractions: Jagat Mandir (main shrine), Rukmini Devi Temple, Gomti Ghat
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Gujarati thali, local sweets like Mohanthal
  • Hotels & Stay: Hotel Gomti, Dwarkadhish Lords Eco Inn
  • Restaurants: AmrutRas, Charmi Restaurant
