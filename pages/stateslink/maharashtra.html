Tourists places in Maharashtra

1. Ajanta and Ellora Caves

Location: Aurangabad district, Maharashtra

Famous For: Ancient rock-cut Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain caves

  • Attractions: Cave paintings, sculptures, UNESCO World Heritage Sites
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Maharashtrian thali, local snacks
  • Hotels & Stay: Hotels in Aurangabad city
  • Restaurants: Local and multi-cuisine restaurants in Aurangabad
  • Why Famous: Exceptional ancient art and architecture showcasing religious heritage
