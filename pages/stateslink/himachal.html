Tourist Places in HimachalPradesh

1. Manali

Location: Kullu District

Famous For: Snow-capped mountains, adventure sports, and scenic beauty

  • Attractions: Solang Valley, Hidimba Devi Temple, Rohtang Pass
  • Best Time to Visit: October to June
  • Famous Food: Sidu, Trout fish, Dham
  • Hotels & Stay: The Himalayan, Snow Valley Resorts
  • Restaurants: Johnson’s Cafe, Chopsticks
  • Why Famous: A popular honeymoon and backpacker destination offering snow adventures

2. Shimla

Location: Capital city of Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Colonial architecture, scenic views, and toy train

  • Attractions: Mall Road, Jakhoo Temple, Kufri
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June and December to January
  • Famous Food: Channa Madra, Babru, Chicken Anardana
  • Hotels & Stay: Clarkes Hotel, Radisson Hotel Shimla
  • Restaurants: Wake & Bake Cafe, Indian Coffee House
  • Why Famous: Once the summer capital of British India, known for its pleasant climate

3. Dharamshala

Location: Kangra District

Famous For: Tibetan culture and monasteries

  • Attractions: Dalai Lama Temple, Bhagsunag Waterfall, Dharamkot
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June, September to December
  • Famous Food: Tibetan momos, Thukpa
  • Hotels & Stay: Fortune Park Moksha, Hotel Norbu House
  • Restaurants: Tibet Kitchen, Jimmy’s Italian Kitchen
  • Why Famous: Residence of the Dalai Lama and serene natural surroundings

4. Kullu

Location: Beside Beas River

Famous For: Temples, river rafting, and Dussehra festival

  • Attractions: Great Himalayan National Park, Bijli Mahadev Temple
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June and September to November
  • Famous Food: Tudkiya Bhath, Dham
  • Hotels & Stay: Himalayan Village Resort, Apple Valley Resort
  • Restaurants: Valentino’s, Little Italy
  • Why Famous: Adventure activities and rich cultural festivals

5. Spiti Valley

Location: Lahaul and Spiti District

Famous For: Cold desert landscape, monasteries, and trekking

  • Attractions: Key Monastery, Chandratal Lake, Dhankar Monastery
  • Best Time to Visit: May to October
  • Famous Food: Thukpa, Butter Tea
  • Hotels & Stay: Zostel Spiti, Hotel Deyzor
  • Restaurants: Sol Cafe, Dragon Restaurant
  • Why Famous: Remote Himalayan region with stark natural beauty and unique Tibetan culture

6. Dalhousie

Location: Chamba District

Famous For: Colonial charm and natural beauty

  • Attractions: Panchpula, Khajjiar, Dainkund Peak
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June
  • Famous Food: Madra, Chana Bhatura
  • Hotels & Stay: Grand View Hotel, Alps Resort
  • Restaurants: Kwality Restaurant, Old Sher-e-Punjab
  • Why Famous: Known as 'Mini Switzerland of India' for its alpine meadows

7. Kasol

Location: Parvati Valley, Kullu

Famous For: Backpacking and Israeli culture

  • Attractions: Chalal, Manikaran Sahib, Parvati River
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June and September to November
  • Famous Food: Falafel, Shakshouka, Pizza
  • Hotels & Stay: The Hosteller Kasol, Parvati Kuteer
  • Restaurants: Evergreen Cafe, Moon Dance Cafe
  • Why Famous: Ideal for trekking, hippie culture, and scenic camping

8. Mcleodganj

Location: Suburb of Dharamshala

Famous For: Tibetan culture and spiritual centers

  • Attractions: Namgyal Monastery, Bhagsunath Temple, Triund Trek
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June and September to November
  • Famous Food: Tibetan momos, Thukpa
  • Hotels & Stay: Pink House, Hotel Bhagsu
  • Restaurants: Tibet Kitchen, Nick’s Italian Kitchen
  • Why Famous: Center of Tibetan Buddhism and meditation retreats

9. Kasauli

Location: Solan District

Famous For: Tranquil environment and colonial charm

  • Attractions: Christ Church, Monkey Point, Gilbert Trail
  • Best Time to Visit: April to June and September to November
  • Famous Food: Sidu, Bun samosa
  • Hotels & Stay: Kasauli Regency, Baikunth Resort
  • Restaurants: Hangout Rooftop Bar, Cafe Mitti
  • Why Famous: A quiet hill station ideal for nature walks and relaxation

10. Bir Billing

Location: Joginder Nagar Valley

Famous For: Paragliding capital of India

  • Attractions: Paragliding, Tibetan monasteries, Bir Tea Factory
  • Best Time to Visit: April to June and October to November
  • Famous Food: Tibetan momos, Noodles
  • Hotels & Stay: The Bunker Bir, Zostel Bir
  • Restaurants: Garden Cafe, Musafir Cafe
  • Why Famous: World-renowned paragliding site with scenic views

11. Chail

Location: Near Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Serene environment and highest cricket ground

  • Attractions: Chail Palace, Kali Ka Tibba, Chail Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June and September to November
  • Famous Food: Chana Madra, Siddu
  • Hotels & Stay: Tarika Resort & Spa, Fernhill Resort
  • Restaurants: Chail Palace Restaurant, Tarika Jungle Retreat Dining
  • Why Famous: Peaceful hill station with lush greenery and historical royal residence

12. Khajjiar

Location: Near Dalhousie, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Mini Switzerland of India

  • Attractions: Khajjiar Lake, Khajji Nag Temple, Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Best Time to Visit: May to October
  • Famous Food: Rajma chawal, Madra
  • Hotels & Stay: Royal Residency, Hotel Mini Swiss
  • Restaurants: Devdar Restaurant, Hotel Mini Swiss Restaurant
  • Why Famous: Scenic meadows surrounded by forests, often compared to Switzerland

13. Kufri

Location: Near Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Snow sports and panoramic views

  • Attractions: Kufri Fun World, Mahasu Peak, Himalayan Nature Park
  • Best Time to Visit: December to February (for snow)
  • Famous Food: Maggi, Siddu
  • Hotels & Stay: Snow King Retreat, Sterling Kufri
  • Restaurants: Cafe Lalit, Snow View Restaurant
  • Why Famous: A favorite destination for skiing and tobogganing during winter

14. Parvati Valley

Location: Kullu District, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Spiritual retreats and trekking trails

  • Attractions: Kasol, Manikaran, Tosh, Kheerganga Trek
  • Best Time to Visit: April to October
  • Famous Food: Israeli cuisine, local Himachali dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: Parvati Kuteer, The Himalayan Village
  • Restaurants: Moon Dance Cafe, Stone Garden Cafe
  • Why Famous: Known for scenic beauty, hippie culture, and adventure trails

15. Tirthan Valley

Location: Kullu District, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Eco-tourism and trout fishing

  • Attractions: Great Himalayan National Park, Jalori Pass, Serolsar Lake
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June and October to November
  • Famous Food: Trout fish, Siddu
  • Hotels & Stay: Raju Bharti Guest House, Tirthan Anglers' Retreat
  • Restaurants: Local homestays and cafes
  • Why Famous: A peaceful destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers

16. Jibhi

Location: Banjar Valley, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Offbeat scenic village with traditional wooden houses

  • Attractions: Jibhi Waterfall, Chehni Kothi, Shringa Rishi Temple
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June and September to November
  • Famous Food: Siddu, local Himachali dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: The Forest Edge, Latoda Eco Lodge
  • Restaurants: Cafe Jungle Valley, Hari Om Cafe
  • Why Famous: A tranquil hamlet perfect for peaceful nature retreats

17. Sethan Valley

Location: Near Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Snow adventures and Buddhist culture

  • Attractions: Igloo stays, snow treks, panoramic views of Dhauladhar ranges
  • Best Time to Visit: December to March (snow), May to June (summer)
  • Famous Food: Tibetan and Himachali dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: Igloo Village Manali, Sethan Heights
  • Restaurants: Home-cooked meals at homestays
  • Why Famous: Unique winter igloo experience and serene Buddhist village life

18. Kaza

Location: Spiti Valley, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Monasteries and high-altitude desert landscape

  • Attractions: Key Monastery, Kibber Village, Hikkim (world’s highest post office)
  • Best Time to Visit: May to October
  • Famous Food: Momos, thukpa, Tibetan bread
  • Hotels & Stay: Hotel Deyzor, The Travellers Shed
  • Restaurants: Himalayan Cafe, Sol Cafe
  • Why Famous: Gateway to Spiti, known for its Buddhist heritage and stark beauty

19. Triund

Location: Near Mcleodganj, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Trekking and scenic Himalayan views

  • Attractions: Triund Trek, Dhauladhar mountain range, stargazing
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June and September to November
  • Famous Food: Tea, Maggi, snacks from trek-side stalls
  • Hotels & Stay: Campsites and lodges in Mcleodganj
  • Restaurants: Local cafes in Mcleodganj
  • Why Famous: One of the most popular easy treks in Himachal with breathtaking views

20. Malana

Location: Parvati Valley, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Ancient culture and distinct traditions

  • Attractions: Jamlu Devta Temple, scenic trekking trails
  • Best Time to Visit: May to August
  • Famous Food: Local Himachali dishes prepared by homestays
  • Hotels & Stay: Local guest houses and camping sites
  • Restaurants: Limited eateries; food usually provided at homestays
  • Why Famous: Isolated village with a unique culture and legal system, known for Malana Cream

21. Tosh

Location: Parvati Valley, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Hippie culture and beautiful mountain views

  • Attractions: Parvati River, forest trails, serene village life
  • Best Time to Visit: April to October
  • Famous Food: Israeli and local Himachali cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Pink Floyd Cafe and Guesthouse, Whoopers Hostel
  • Restaurants: Hill Top Cafe, Pink Floyd Cafe
  • Why Famous: Peaceful escape for trekkers and backpackers seeking raw nature

22. Palampur

Location: Kangra Valley, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Tea gardens and colonial charm

  • Attractions: Saurabh Van Vihar, Neugal Khad, tea estates
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June, September to November
  • Famous Food: Momos, thukpa, Himachali thali
  • Hotels & Stay: Hotel Norwood Green, RS Sarovar Portico
  • Restaurants: Sky Room Bar & Restaurant, Cafe Coffee Day Palampur
  • Why Famous: Known as the 'Tea Capital of North India', surrounded by pine forests and mountains

23. Narkanda

Location: Shimla district, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Skiing and apple orchards

  • Attractions: Hatu Peak, Stokes Farm, skiing slopes
  • Best Time to Visit: December to February (for snow), April to June (for greenery)
  • Famous Food: Local Himachali dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: Agyaat Vaas, The Wilderness
  • Restaurants: Him Cafe, local dhabas
  • Why Famous: Winter sports destination with panoramic views of the Himalayas

24. Prashar Lake Trek

Location: Mandi district, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Alpine lake and temple with floating island

  • Attractions: Prashar Lake, Parashar Rishi Temple, surrounding Dhauladhar views
  • Best Time to Visit: April to June and September to November
  • Famous Food: Simple trek meals and Himachali dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: Campsites, forest guest house
  • Restaurants: Basic food stalls and dhabas near the lake
  • Why Famous: Mysterious lake with a floating island and tranquil beauty

25. Sissu

Location: Lahaul Valley, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Scenic landscapes and adventure tourism

  • Attractions: Sissu Waterfall, Sissu Lake, suspension bridge, Atal Tunnel access
  • Best Time to Visit: May to October
  • Famous Food: Tibetan cuisine and local dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: Nomad's Cottage, The Sissu Retreat
  • Restaurants: Local eateries and cafes
  • Why Famous: Popular stopover for travelers heading towards Leh via Atal Tunnel

26. Naggar

Famous For: Ancient castle, art gallery, and scenic beauty

  • Attractions: Naggar Castle, Nicholas Roerich Art Gallery, Tripura Sundari Temple
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June and September to November
  • Famous Food: Himachali siddu, local thali
  • Hotels & Stay: Naggar Castle Hotel, Soham’s Chateau De Naggar
  • Restaurants: Nightingale Restaurant, Hotel Sheetal
  • Why Famous: Blend of history, architecture, and peaceful views of the Beas Valley

27. Sarahan

Famous For: Bhimakali Temple and apple orchards

  • Famous Food: Traditional Himachali food

28. Jispa

29. Kangra

Location: Kangra district, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Ancient temples and forts

  • Attractions: Kangra Fort, Bajreshwari Devi Temple, Masroor Rock Cut Temples
  • Best Time to Visit: September to June
  • Famous Food: Chha Gosht, kangri dham
  • Hotels & Stay: WelcomHeritage Judges Court, Hotel Kangra Rodeway Inn
  • Restaurants: Local eateries, traditional Himachali food joints
  • Why Famous: One of the oldest hill cities with deep spiritual and historical significance

30. Keylong

Location: Lahaul and Spiti district, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Administrative center of Lahaul, Buddhist monasteries

  • Attractions: Shashur Monastery, Kardang Monastery, Gemur Monastery
  • Best Time to Visit: June to September
  • Famous Food: Thukpa, momos, local Himalayan food
  • Hotels & Stay: Dekyid Hotel, Hotel Triveni
  • Restaurants: Basic eateries, hotel dining
  • Why Famous: Remote mountain town surrounded by monasteries and rivers

31. Rohtang Pass

Location: Near Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Snow adventure and mountain pass connecting Kullu to Lahaul-Spiti

  • Attractions: Snow biking, skiing, panoramic Himalayan views
  • Best Time to Visit: May to October (closed in winter due to snowfall)
  • Famous Food: Maggi, tea stalls, local food from dhabas
  • Hotels & Stay: Stay in Manali (no accommodation at the pass)
  • Restaurants: Roadside dhabas near the pass and in Manali
  • Why Famous: Popular destination for snow sports and breathtaking landscapes

Famous For: Peaceful hill retreat with pine forests

  • Attractions: Tara Devi Temple, camping, nature walks
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June and September to November
  • Famous Food: Himachali thali, siddu
  • Hotels & Stay: Aamod at Shoghi, Oakwood Hamlet
  • Restaurants: Hotel restaurants, nature resort cafés
  • Why Famous: Offbeat destination for quiet vacations and nature lovers

33. Chamba

Famous For: Ancient temples and scenic beauty

  • Best Time to Visit: April to June and September to December

34. Tattapani

Location: Near Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Natural hot springs on the banks of Sutlej River

  • Attractions: Hot water springs, river rafting, Mahadev Cave Temple
  • Best Time to Visit: October to June
  • Famous Food: Local Himachali dishes, snacks
  • Hotels & Stay: Hot Spring Therme & Spa, Hotel New Spring View
  • Restaurants: Hotel restaurants and local eateries
  • Why Famous: Known for therapeutic hot springs and peaceful surroundings

35. Indrahar Pass Trek

Location: Starts from Mcleodganj, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Scenic and thrilling high-altitude trek in the Dhauladhar range

  • Attractions: Triund, Lahesh Caves, views of Pir Panjal range
  • Best Time to Visit: May to October
  • Famous Food: Food carried by trekkers or served at base camps
  • Hotels & Stay: Stay at Mcleodganj or Dharamkot before/after trek
  • Restaurants: Cafés and eateries in Mcleodganj
  • Why Famous: One of the most scenic and popular treks in Himachal with alpine views

36. Manikaran Sahib

Location: Parvati Valley, near Kasol, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Sacred pilgrimage site for Sikhs and Hindus

  • Attractions: Manikaran Gurudwara, hot springs, Ram Temple
  • Best Time to Visit: April to June and September to November
  • Famous Food: Langar (community kitchen), local Himachali food
  • Hotels & Stay: Parvati Kuteer, Hotel Sandhya Kasol
  • Restaurants: Evergreen Café, Moon Dance Café (near Kasol)
  • Why Famous: Religious significance and natural hot springs

38. Shoja

Famous For: Offbeat destination with lush greenery

  • Best Time to Visit: April to June and September to November
  • Famous Food: Siddu, momos, local cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Banjara Retreat, The Forest Edge

  • Why Famous: Perfect for solitude seekers and nature lovers

39. Hidimba Devi Temple

Location: Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Famous For: Ancient cave temple surrounded by cedar forest

  • Attractions: Temple architecture, spiritual ambiance, cedar forest walk
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June and October to February
  • Famous Food: Local sweets and snacks from Manali
  • Hotels & Stay: Snow Valley Resorts, Apple Country Resort
  • Restaurants: Johnson’s Café, Il Forno
  • Why Famous: Mythological significance and peaceful setting

40. Jogini Waterfall

Location: Near Vashisht Village, Manali

Famous For: Serene trekking spot with beautiful waterfall

  • Attractions: Waterfall, trekking through pine forests, valley views
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June and September to November
  • Famous Food: Tea and snacks at small stalls on the trail
  • Hotels & Stay: Stay in Manali town
  • Restaurants: Local cafés in Vashisht and Manali
  • Why Famous: Perfect for nature lovers and short treks
