Tourist Places in Chhattisgarh

1. Raipur

Location: Capital city of Chhattisgarh

Famous For: Modern city with cultural and historical sites

  • Attractions: Mahant Ghasidas Museum, Vivekanand Sarovar, Purkhauti Muktangan
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Famous Food: Chhattisgarhi dishes like Chila, Faraa
  • Hotels: Hotel Babylon International, Courtyard by Marriott
  • Restaurants: Swad Restaurant, Dosa Plaza
  • Why Famous: Gateway to Chhattisgarh with rich tribal culture

2. Barnawapara Wildlife Sanctuary

Location: Near Raipur

Famous For: Rich biodiversity and wildlife safaris

  • Attractions: Tigers, leopards, deer, birdwatching
  • Best Time: November to June
  • Famous Food: Local tribal cuisine nearby
  • Hotels: Forest rest houses and lodges
  • Restaurants: Limited, mostly near Raipur
  • Why Famous: Important sanctuary for tiger conservation

3. Jagdalpur

Location: Bastar district

Famous For: Tribal culture, handicrafts, and natural beauty

  • Attractions: Chitrakote Falls, Bastar Palace, local markets
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Famous Food: Bastar tribal cuisine, bamboo chicken
  • Hotels: Hotel New Palace, Tiger Falls Resort
  • Restaurants: Bastar Dine, Jungle Treat
  • Why Famous: Cultural hub of Bastar and gateway to waterfalls

4. Charre Marre Waterfalls

Location: Near Jagdalpur

Famous For: Scenic waterfall amidst dense forest

  • Attractions: Waterfalls, trekking, nature walks
  • Best Time: Monsoon and post-monsoon (July to October)
  • Famous Food: Local tribal snacks
  • Hotels: Nearby Jagdalpur hotels
  • Restaurants: Local eateries in Jagdalpur
  • Why Famous: Picturesque waterfalls with serene environment
