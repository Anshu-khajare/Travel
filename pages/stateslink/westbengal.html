Tourist Places in West Bengal

1. Kolkata

Location: Capital city of West Bengal

Famous For: Colonial architecture, cultural heritage, Howrah Bridge

  • Attractions: Victoria Memorial, Indian Museum, Dakshineswar Kali Temple, Howrah Bridge
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Famous Food: Rosogolla, Kathi rolls, Fish curry
  • Hotels: The Oberoi Grand, Taj Bengal, ITC Sonar
  • Restaurants: Peter Cat, Oh! Calcutta, 6 Ballygunge Place

2. Darjeeling

Location: Darjeeling district, northern West Bengal

Famous For: Tea gardens, Himalayan views, Toy Train

  • Attractions: Tiger Hill sunrise, Batasia Loop, Darjeeling Himalayan Railway
  • Best Time: March to May and October to November
  • Famous Food: Momos, Thukpa, local Tibetan dishes
  • Hotels: Mayfair Darjeeling, Glenburn Tea Estate, Summit Grace
  • Restaurants: Kunga Restaurant, Glenary's Bakery

3. Sundarbans

Location: Southern part of West Bengal, mangrove forest

Famous For: Largest mangrove forest, Royal Bengal Tigers

  • Attractions: Tiger safari, boating, wildlife sanctuary
  • Best Time: November to February
  • Famous Food: Freshwater fish dishes
  • Hotels: Sunderban Tiger Camp, Forest Rest Houses
  • Restaurants: Limited; mostly hotel dining

4. Siliguri

Location: Darjeeling district, gateway to Northeast India

Famous For: Trade hub, access point to hill stations and Northeast

  • Attractions: Coronation Bridge, North Bengal Science Centre
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Famous Food: North Indian, Bengali snacks
  • Hotels: The Elgin Silver Oaks, Hotel Sinclairs
  • Restaurants: Shangri-La, Taste of Tibet

5. Mirik

Location: Darjeeling district

Famous For: Beautiful lake, tea gardens

  • Attractions: Sumendu Lake, Buddhist Monastery
  • Best Time: March to May and October to December
  • Famous Food: Local Nepali cuisine, momos
  • Hotels: Hotel Mirik Hill Resort, Pinewood Resort
  • Restaurants: Hotel Snow Lion, local eateries

6. Digha

Location: East Midnapore district, coastal town

  • Attractions: Digha Beach, New Digha Beach
