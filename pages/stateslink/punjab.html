Tourist Places in Punjab

1. Golden Temple

Location: Amritsar

Famous For: Sikh spiritual site and architecture

  • Attractions: Harmandir Sahib, Amrit Sarovar
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Langar, Amritsari Kulcha
  • Hotels & Stay: Hyatt Regency, Ramada Amritsar
  • Restaurants: Bharawan Da Dhaba, Kesar Da Dhaba
  • Why Famous: Most important religious site for Sikhs

2. Wagha Border

Location: India-Pakistan Border, near Amritsar

Famous For: Daily border ceremony

  • Attractions: Beating Retreat Ceremony
  • Best Time to Visit: Year-round, evening time for ceremony
  • Famous Food: Street snacks near the site
  • Hotels & Stay: Nearby Amritsar hotels
  • Restaurants: Roadside eateries
  • Why Famous: Patriotic military ceremony between India and Pakistan

3. Jallianwala Bagh

Location: Amritsar

Famous For: Historical massacre site

  • Attractions: Martyrs’ Well, Memorial Flame
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Local Amritsari cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Nearby heritage hotels in Amritsar
  • Restaurants: Local eateries around heritage street
  • Why Famous: Site of 1919 British massacre

4. The Partition Museum

Location: Town Hall, Amritsar

Famous For: India's only museum dedicated to Partition

  • Attractions: Oral histories, exhibits, photographs
  • Best Time to Visit: Throughout the year
  • Famous Food: Amritsari food at nearby cafes
  • Hotels & Stay: Hotels near Town Hall or Golden Temple
  • Restaurants: Partition Cafe, Svaagat
  • Why Famous: Powerful retelling of Partition’s human impact

5. Durgiana Temple

Location: Amritsar

Famous For: Hindu temple resembling Golden Temple

  • Attractions: Temple architecture, Durga and Lakshmi idols
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Prasad, local sweets
  • Hotels & Stay: Nearby hotels in city center
  • Restaurants: Vegetarian eateries
  • Why Famous: Hindu religious site with unique architecture

10. Anandpur Sahib

Location: Rupnagar District, Punjab

Famous For: One of the most sacred places in Sikhism

  • Attractions: Takht Sri Kesgarh Sahib, Virasat-e-Khalsa Museum
  • Best Time to Visit: November to March
  • Famous Food: Punjabi thali, lassi
  • Hotels & Stay: Anand At The Satluj, local guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Giani Tea Stall, Punjabi dhabas
  • Why Famous: Birthplace of Khalsa; significant Sikh pilgrimage site

11. Pathankot

Location: Northern tip of Punjab

Famous For: Gateway to Himachal and Jammu

  • Attractions: Ranjit Sagar Dam, Nurpur Fort (nearby)
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Rajma Chawal, North Indian cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Hotel Venice, The Grand Hotel
  • Restaurants: Pahari Dhaba, Mezbaan Restaurant
  • Why Famous: Transit city to hill stations, scenic riverside

12. Gurudwara Ber Sahib

Location: Sultanpur Lodhi, Punjab

Famous For: Historical Sikh shrine

  • Attractions: Gurudwara Ber Sahib, other nearby gurudwaras
  • Best Time to Visit: November (Guru Nanak Jayanti)
  • Famous Food: Langar, traditional Punjabi meals
  • Hotels & Stay: Pilgrim inns, guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Local dhabas
  • Why Famous: Associated with Guru Nanak Dev Ji's enlightenment

13. Bathinda

Location: Western Punjab

Famous For: Historic forts and thermal power stations

  • Attractions: Qila Mubarak, Rose Garden, Thermal Plant
  • Best Time to Visit: October to February
  • Famous Food: Amritsari Kulcha, local Punjabi cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Comfort Inn Tulip Heights, Stella Hotel
  • Restaurants: Food Garage, Punjabi Zaika
  • Why Famous: One of the oldest cities in Punjab with rich heritage

14. Ropar Wetlands (Rupnagar Wetlands)

Location: Rupnagar District, Punjab

Famous For: Birdwatching, natural biodiversity

  • Attractions: Migratory birds, eco-trails, boating
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Street food near Rupnagar
  • Hotels & Stay: Ropar Guesthouses, Nature Resorts
  • Restaurants: Nearby local eateries
  • Why Famous: Ramsar site; serene nature reserve and bird habitat

15. Qila Mubarak, Bathinda

Location: Bathinda, Punjab

Famous For: One of the oldest forts in India

  • Attractions: Ancient architecture, Razia Sultan’s connection, historical displays
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Punjabi thali, Bathinda-style kulcha
  • Hotels & Stay: Hotel Sepal, Stella Hotel
  • Restaurants: Rangrezza Restaurant, Punjabi Rasoi
  • Why Famous: Razia Sultan, the first female ruler of Delhi, was imprisoned here

16. Sheesh Mahal, Patiala

Location: Patiala, Punjab

Famous For: Glasswork architecture and royal museum collection

  • Attractions: Mirror artwork, museum with paintings and manuscripts
  • Best Time to Visit: October to February
  • Famous Food: Patiala Shahi Paneer, tandoori dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: Neemrana’s Baradari Palace, Hotel Mohan Continental
  • Restaurants: The Patiala Kitchen, Sahni's Dhaba
  • Why Famous: A grand display of royal Patiala architecture and culture

17. Gobindgarh Fort, Amritsar

Location: Amritsar, Punjab

Famous For: Cultural and entertainment complex in a historic fort

  • Attractions: 7D shows, museums, light & sound shows, cultural performances
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Local street food, Amritsari Kulcha
  • Hotels & Stay: Ramada, Hyatt Amritsar
  • Restaurants: Brothers Dhaba, Crystal Restaurant
  • Why Famous: A modern heritage site blending history with interactive experiences

18. Phillaur Fort, Ludhiana

Location: Phillaur, Ludhiana District, Punjab

Famous For: Mughal-style architecture and its current use as a Police Academy

  • Attractions: Mughal-era design, historical military architecture
  • Best Time to Visit: November to February
  • Famous Food: Butter chicken, Ludhiana chaat
  • Hotels & Stay: Hotel City Home, Keys Select Hotel
  • Restaurants: Baba Chicken, Kitchen at 95
  • Why Famous: Historic fortification now serving as Punjab Police Academy
