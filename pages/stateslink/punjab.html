Tourist Places in Punjab

1. Golden Temple

Location: Amritsar

Famous For: Sikh spiritual site and architecture

  • Attractions: Harmandir Sahib, Amrit Sarovar
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Langar, Amritsari Kulcha
  • Hotels & Stay: Hyatt Regency, Ramada Amritsar
  • Restaurants: Bharawan Da Dhaba, Kesar Da Dhaba
  • Why Famous: Most important religious site for Sikhs

2. Wagha Border

Location: India-Pakistan Border, near Amritsar

Famous For: Daily border ceremony

  • Attractions: Beating Retreat Ceremony
  • Best Time to Visit: Year-round, evening time for ceremony
  • Famous Food: Street snacks near the site
  • Hotels & Stay: Nearby Amritsar hotels
  • Restaurants: Roadside eateries
  • Why Famous: Patriotic military ceremony between India and Pakistan

3. Jallianwala Bagh

Location: Amritsar

Famous For: Historical massacre site

  • Attractions: Martyrs’ Well, Memorial Flame
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Local Amritsari cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Nearby heritage hotels in Amritsar
  • Restaurants: Local eateries around heritage street
  • Why Famous: Site of 1919 British massacre

4. The Partition Museum

Location: Town Hall, Amritsar

Famous For: India's only museum dedicated to Partition

  • Attractions: Oral histories, exhibits, photographs
  • Best Time to Visit: Throughout the year
  • Famous Food: Amritsari food at nearby cafes
  • Hotels & Stay: Hotels near Town Hall or Golden Temple
  • Restaurants: Partition Cafe, Svaagat
  • Why Famous: Powerful retelling of Partition’s human impact

5. Durgiana Temple

Location: Amritsar

Famous For: Hindu temple resembling Golden Temple

  • Attractions: Temple architecture, Durga and Lakshmi idols
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Prasad, local sweets
  • Hotels & Stay: Nearby hotels in city center
  • Restaurants: Vegetarian eateries
  • Why Famous: Hindu religious site with unique architecture

6. Ram Tirath Temple

Location: Near Amritsar, Punjab

Famous For: Ancient temple believed to be birthplace of Sage Valmiki’s ashram

  • Attractions: Temple complex, pond, religious fairs
  • Best Time to Visit: Year-round
  • Famous Food: Local vegetarian food
  • Hotels & Stay: Stay in Amritsar
  • Restaurants: Amritsar restaurants
  • Why Famous: Religious significance for Hindus and mythological importance

7. Akal Takht

Location: Golden Temple complex, Amritsar

Famous For: Highest seat of Sikh religious authority

  • Attractions: Historic building, Sikh religious events
  • Best Time to Visit: Year-round
  • Famous Food: Langar at Golden Temple
  • Hotels & Stay: Amritsar hotels
  • Restaurants: Langar and local eateries
  • Why Famous: Central to Sikhism’s religious governance and ceremonies

8. Sadda Pind

Location: Near Amritsar, Punjab

Famous For: Cultural village showcasing Punjabi traditions

  • Attractions: Folk dances, cultural performances, traditional Punjabi cuisine
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Sarson da saag, makki di roti, lassi
  • Hotels & Stay: Day trip from Amritsar
  • Restaurants: On-site traditional dining
  • Why Famous: Immersive Punjabi village experience with lively festivities

9. Chandigarh

Location: Union Territory and capital of Punjab and Haryana

Famous For: Planned city with modern architecture and gardens

  • Attractions: Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake, Rose Garden, Capitol Complex
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Chole bhature, butter chicken, street food
  • Hotels & Stay: Taj Chandigarh, JW Marriott
  • Restaurants: Black Lotus, Pal Dhaba
  • Why Famous: Unique urban design by Le Corbusier and lush green spaces

10. Amritsar

Location: Northwestern Punjab

Famous For: Spirituality, history, and Punjabi culture

  • Attractions: Golden Temple, Jallianwala Bagh, Wagha Border
  • Best Time to Visit: November to March
  • Famous Food: Amritsari kulcha, chole, lassi
  • Hotels & Stay: Hyatt Amritsar, Hotel Ritz Plaza
  • Restaurants: Kesar Da Dhaba, Bharawan Da Dhaba
  • Why Famous: Rich heritage, religious sites, vibrant bazaars

11. Patiala

Location: Southeastern Punjab

Famous For: Royal heritage and traditional crafts

  • Attractions: Qila Mubarak, Sheesh Mahal, Baradari Gardens
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Patiala lassi, Amritsari kulcha
  • Hotels & Stay: Hotel Park Avenue, Hotel KLG International
  • Restaurants: Barbeque Nation, Balle Balle
  • Why Famous: Rich royal architecture and vibrant Punjabi culture
