Tourist Places in Nagaland

1. Dzukou Valley

Location: On the border of Nagaland and Manipur

Famous For: Scenic valley, rare flora including the Dzukou Lily

  • Attractions: Trekking, nature walks, camping
  • Best Time to Visit: June to September and November to February
  • Famous Food: Local Naga cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Campsites and homestays in nearby villages
  • Restaurants: Local eateries in nearby Kohima and Viswema
  • Why Famous: Pristine natural beauty and biodiversity hotspot
