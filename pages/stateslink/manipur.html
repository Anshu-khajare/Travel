Tourist Places in Manipur

1. Kangla Fort

Location: Imphal

Famous For: Historical royal palace of Manipur kings

  • Attractions: Ancient palace ruins, temples, cultural heritage
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Manipuri cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Imphal hotels and guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Local Manipuri eateries
  • Why Famous: Cultural and historical center of Manipur

2. Loktak Lake

Location: Moirang, Bishnupur district

Famous For: Largest freshwater lake in Northeast India

  • Attractions: Floating phumdis (vegetation islands), boating, nature walks
  • Best Time to Visit: November to March
  • Famous Food: Freshwater fish dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: Lakeside resorts and guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Local restaurants serving fish specialties
  • Why Famous: Unique ecosystem and natural beauty

  • Restaurants: Local vegetarian eateries
  • Why Famous: Important religious and cultural site

4. Imphal War Cemetery
