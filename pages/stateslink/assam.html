Tourist Places in Assam

1. Kaziranga National Park

Location: Golaghat and Nagaon districts

Famous For: One-horned Indian rhinoceros and rich wildlife

  • Attractions: Safari rides, rhino spotting, bird watching
  • Best Time to Visit: November to April
  • Famous Food: Assamese cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Resorts and lodges near park entrances
  • Restaurants: Local and resort dining
  • Why Famous: UNESCO World Heritage site and biodiversity hotspot

2. Guwahati

Location: Kamrup Metropolitan district

Famous For: Gateway to Northeast India, temples, river Brahmaputra

  • Attractions: Kamakhya Temple, Umananda Island, Assam State Museum
  • Best Time to Visit: October to April
  • Famous Food: Assamese and North-East Indian dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: Wide range from budget to luxury
  • Restaurants: Numerous local and multi-cuisine options
  • Why Famous: Cultural hub and vibrant city by Brahmaputra

3. Maa Kamakhya Temple

Location: Nilachal Hill, Guwahati

Famous For: One of the oldest Shakti Peethas in India

  • Attractions: Temple architecture, Ambubachi Mela festival
  • Best Time to Visit: October to April
  • Famous Food: Prasad and local Assamese snacks
  • Hotels & Stay: Many in Guwahati city
  • Restaurants: Nearby eateries serving traditional food
  • Why Famous: Major pilgrimage site for Shaktism

4. Majuli

Location: River island in Brahmaputra River

Famous For: World's largest river island, vibrant Assamese culture

  • Attractions: Satras (Vaishnavite monasteries), traditional festivals
  • Best Time to Visit: November to March
  • Famous Food: Traditional Assamese cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Homestays and guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Local kitchens
  • Why Famous: Rich cultural heritage and scenic beauty

5. Umananda Island

Location: Brahmaputra River, Guwahati

Famous For: Small river island with Umananda Temple

  • Attractions: Temple visits, river views, birdwatching
  • Best Time to Visit: October to April
  • Famous Food: Snacks from Guwahati markets
  • Hotels & Stay: Stay in Guwahati city
  • Restaurants: Onshore eateries
  • Why Famous: Smallest inhabited river island in the world
