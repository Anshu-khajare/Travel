Rajasthan tourists places

1. Jaipur

Famous For: Pink City, royal heritage

  • Attractions: Amer Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, Jantar Mantar
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Famous Food: Dal Baati Churma, Ghewar
  • Hotels & Stay: Heritage hotels, palaces, budget hotels
  • Restaurants: Local Rajasthani cuisine, rooftop cafes
  • Why Famous: Rich culture, architecture, vibrant bazaars
