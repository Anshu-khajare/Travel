Tourist Places in Meghalaya

1. Shillong

Location: Capital city of Meghalaya

Famous For: Scenic hill station, colonial architecture

  • Attractions: Wards Lake, Shillong Peak, Lady Hydari Park
  • Best Time to Visit: October to May
  • Famous Food: Jadoh, Tungrymbai
  • Hotels & Stay: Various hotels, guesthouses, homestays
  • Restaurants: Local and multi-cuisine restaurants
  • Why Famous: "Scotland of the East", cultural and natural beauty

2. Double Decker Living Root Bridge

Location: Nongriat village, near Cherrapunji

Famous For: Unique double-layered living root bridge

  • Attractions: Trekking, natural architecture
  • Best Time to Visit: October to May
  • Famous Food: Local Khasi delicacies
  • Hotels & Stay: Homestays in Nongriat and nearby villages
  • Restaurants: Village kitchens
  • Why Famous: One of the few double-decker root bridges in the world

3. Dawki

Location: West Jaintia Hills district

Famous For: Clear Umngot River and Indo-Bangladesh border

  • Attractions: River boating, border market
  • Best Time to Visit: October to April
  • Famous Food: Fresh river fish dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: Guesthouses in Dawki and nearby
  • Restaurants: Local eateries
  • Why Famous: Crystal clear river and picturesque border area

4. Mawsynram

Location: East Khasi Hills district

Famous For: Wettest place on Earth

  • Attractions: Waterfalls, caves, lush greenery
  • Best Time to Visit: October to April (to avoid heavy rain)
  • Famous Food: Khasi tribal cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Guesthouses and small hotels
  • Restaurants: Local diners
  • Why Famous: Extreme rainfall, rich biodiversity

5. Umngot River

Location: Dawki, West Jaintia Hills

Famous For: Crystal-clear river water

  • Attractions: Boating, fishing, scenic views
  • Best Time to Visit: October to April
  • Famous Food: Freshwater fish delicacies
  • Hotels & Stay: Guesthouses in Dawki
  • Restaurants: Local eateries
  • Why Famous: Often called the clearest river in Asia

7. Nohsngithiang Falls (Seven Sisters Falls)

Location: East Khasi Hills district, near Cherrapunji
