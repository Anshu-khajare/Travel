Tourist Places in Sikkim

1. Gangtok (Capital City)

Location: East Sikkim

Famous For: Scenic hill station with vibrant markets and cultural blend

  • Attractions: MG Marg, Rumtek Monastery, Himalayan Zoological Park, Do Drul Chorten
  • Best Time to Visit: March to June, September to December
  • Famous Food: Momos, Thukpa, Gundruk Soup
  • Hotels & Stay: Hotels, guesthouses, homestays
  • Restaurants: Local eateries, cafes on MG Marg
  • Why Famous: Gateway to Sikkim, rich cultural experiences, panoramic mountain views

2. Tsomgo Lake (Changu Lake)

Location: Near Gangtok, East Sikkim

Famous For: Glacial lake with serene beauty and yak rides

  • Attractions: Lake sightseeing, yak rides, local Tibetan culture
  • Best Time to Visit: April to June, September to December
  • Famous Food: Local snacks at nearby stalls
  • Hotels & Stay: Stay in Gangtok
  • Restaurants: Local eateries in Gangtok
  • Why Famous: Stunning high-altitude lake and snow-capped surroundings

3. Nathula Pass

Location: East Sikkim, on Indo-China border

Famous For: High-altitude mountain pass and historic trade route

  • Attractions: Border views, mountain scenery, history
  • Best Time to Visit: May to October (closed in winter)
  • Famous Food: Local street food in nearby towns
  • Hotels & Stay: Gangtok hotels
  • Restaurants: Local eateries in Gangtok
  • Why Famous: Strategic military and historic importance, scenic beauty

4. Yumthang Valley

Location: North Sikkim

  • Restaurants: Local eateries in Lachung
