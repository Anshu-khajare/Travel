Tourist Places in Arunachal Pradesh

1. Tawang

Location: Northwestern Arunachal Pradesh, near Bhutan and China border

Famous For: Tawang Monastery, scenic Himalayan views

  • Attractions: Tawang Monastery, War Memorial, Madhuri Lake
  • Best Time to Visit: April to October
  • Famous Food: Thukpa, Momos, local Tibetan dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: Hotel Gakyi, Hotel Taktsang
  • Restaurants: Namaste Cafe, Sonam's Kitchen
  • Why Famous: One of the largest Buddhist monasteries in India, beautiful landscapes

2. Dirang

Location: West Kameng district, along the Dirang River

Famous For: Hot springs, scenic valleys, apple orchards

  • Attractions: Dirang Hot Springs, Dirang Dzong, Orchards
  • Best Time to Visit: March to October
  • Famous Food: Trout fish, local Arunachali dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: Hotel Monyak, Dirang Riverside Resort
  • Restaurants: Local eateries and cafes
  • Why Famous: Known for natural hot springs and serene valley views

3. Bomdila

Location: West Kameng district

Famous For: Buddhist monasteries, apple orchards, panoramic views

  • Attractions: Bomdila Monastery, Craft Centre, viewpoints
  • Best Time to Visit: April to October
  • Famous Food: Local bamboo shoot dishes, traditional Arunachali food
  • Hotels & Stay: Bomdila Hotel, Hotel New Bomdila
  • Restaurants: Local dhabas and cafes
  • Why Famous: Gateway to Tawang with beautiful monasteries and views

4. Anini

Location: Dibang Valley district, near the Indo-China border

Famous For: Remote tribal culture, scenic beauty

  • Attractions: Dibang River, tribal villages, wildlife
  • Best Time to Visit: May to October
  • Famous Food: Local tribal cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Limited homestays available
  • Restaurants: Local community kitchens
  • Why Famous: One of the least disturbed tribal regions with pristine nature

5. Bhalukpong

Location: West Kameng district, near Assam border

Famous For: River rafting, wildlife sanctuary

  • Attractions: Pakke Tiger Reserve, Kameng River rafting
  • Best Time to Visit: October to May
  • Famous Food: Assamese and Arunachali cuisine
  • Hotels & Stay: Bhalukpong Tourist Lodge, local guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Local dhabas and small restaurants
  • Why Famous: Gateway to Arunachal with adventure activities and wildlife

6. Ziro

Location: Lower Subansiri district

Famous For: Apatani tribal culture, lush green paddy fields

  • Attractions: Ziro Music Festival, Apatani villages
  • Best Time to Visit: March to October
  • Famous Food: Apatani cuisine, bamboo shoot dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: Ziro Valley Resort, local guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Local eateries serving traditional food
  • Why Famous: Unique tribal culture and scenic terraced fields

7. Sela Pass

Location: Between Tawang and Dirang

Famous For: High altitude mountain pass, snow views

  • Attractions: Snow-capped peaks, Sela Lake
  • Best Time to Visit: May to September (winter heavy snow)
  • Famous Food: Tibetan and local cuisines at nearby towns
  • Hotels & Stay: Limited stay nearby, mostly in Dirang or Tawang
  • Restaurants: Roadside eateries in surrounding towns
  • Why Famous: Scenic and strategic mountain pass with breathtaking views
