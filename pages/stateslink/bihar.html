Tourist Places in Bihar

  1. Bodh Gaya

    2. Location: Gaya district, Bihar, India.

    Famous For: Place of Buddha's enlightenment, Mahabodhi Temple (UNESCO site).

    • Main Attractions: Mahabodhi Temple, Bodhi Tree, Great Buddha Statue
    • Best Time to Visit: October to March
    • Famous Foods: Litti Chokha, Sattu Paratha, Malpua, Bihari Lassi
    • Hotels: Hotel Taj Darbar, The Royal Residency, Bodhgaya Regency Hotel
    • Restaurants: The Great Buddha Restaurant, Be Happy Café, Tibet Om Café

  2. Nalanda

    3. Location: Nalanda district, Bihar, India.

    Famous For: Ancient Nalanda University (World's oldest residential university)

    • Main Attractions: University Ruins, Archaeological Museum, Hiuen Tsang Hall
    • Best Time to Visit: October to March
    • Famous Foods: Litti Chokha, Sattu Pani, Silao Khaja
    • Hotels: Nalanda Residency, Gargee's Hotel, Tathagat International
    • Restaurants: Nalanda Restaurant, Vaishali Café, Buddha Café

  3. Rajgir

    4. Location: Nalanda district, Bihar, India.

    Famous For: Ancient capital of Magadha, Buddhist and Jain sites

    • Main Attractions: Vishwa Shanti Stupa, Hot Springs, Venu Vana
    • Best Time to Visit: October to March
    • Famous Foods: Litti Chokha, Sattu Paratha, Khaja
    • Hotels: Nalanda Regency, Indo Hokke Hotel, Tathagat Vihar
    • Restaurants: Flavours, Gautam Vihar Restaurant, Green Restaurant

    4. Patna

    Location: Capital of Bihar, India.

    Famous For: Ancient Pataliputra, political and cultural hub

    • Main Attractions: Golghar, Patna Museum, Takht Sri Patna Sahib
    • Best Time to Visit: October to March
    • Famous Foods: Khaja, Malpua, Dal Pitha, Ghugni
    • Hotels: Hotel Maurya, Hotel Patliputra Ashok, Hotel Chanakya
    • Restaurants: Bansi Vihar, 17 Degrees, Pind Balluchi

    5. Vaishali

    Location: Vaishali district, Bihar, India.

    Famous For: Ancient republic, associated with Buddha and Mahavira

    • Main Attractions: Ashokan Pillar, Vishwa Shanti Stupa, Abhishek Pushkarini
    • Best Time to Visit: November to February
    • Famous Foods: Litti Chokha, Thekua, Malpua
    • Hotels: Vaishali Residency, Buddha Inn, Gargee Grand
    • Restaurants: Vaishali Restaurant, Green View, Spicy Bite

    6. Vikramshila

    Location: Bhagalpur district, Bihar, India.

    Famous For: Buddhist learning center during Pala dynasty

    • Main Attractions: University Ruins, Monastery, Dolphin Sanctuary
    • Best Time to Visit: October to March
    • Famous Foods: Fish Curry, Ghugni, Tilkut
    • Hotels: Rajhans International, Ashoka Grand, Hotel Vineet
    • Restaurants: Zaika, Spicy Hub, Food Plaza

    7. Kesaria
