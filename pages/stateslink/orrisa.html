Tourist Places in Odisha

1. Puri Beach

Location: Puri, Odisha

Famous For: Sandy beach, sunrise views, and proximity to Jagannath Temple

  • Attractions: Beach activities, sea food stalls, local markets
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Famous Food: Sea food, chhena poda (cheese dessert)
  • Hotels: Mayfair Lagoon, Sterling Puri, Hotel Holiday Resort
  • Restaurants: Wildgrass Restaurant, Dalma Restaurant
  • Why Famous: One of India’s prime beaches and a religious hub due to the Jagannath Temple

2. Bhubaneshwar

Location: Capital city of Odisha

Famous For: Temples and rich cultural heritage

  • Attractions: Lingaraja Temple, Mukteshwar Temple, Udayagiri and Khandagiri Caves
  • Best Time: October to February
  • Famous Food: Odia thali, chhena jalebi
  • Hotels: Mayfair Lagoon, Swosti Premium, Trident Bhubaneswar
  • Restaurants: Dalma, Kanika Restaurant
  • Why Famous: Known as the "Temple City of India" with beautiful architecture

3. Konark

Location: Puri district, Odisha

Famous For: The Sun Temple (a UNESCO World Heritage Site)

  • Attractions: Sun Temple, Konark Beach
  • Best Time: October to February
  • Famous Food: Coastal Odia cuisine, seafood
  • Hotels: The New Marrion, Konark Sarovar Portico
  • Restaurants: Chowpati, Konark Inn
  • Why Famous: Architectural marvel representing the chariot of the Sun God

4. Chilika Lake

Location: Coastal Odisha

Famous For: Asia’s largest brackish water lagoon, bird sanctuary

  • Attractions: Bird watching, dolphin sighting, boat rides
  • Best Time: November to February
  • Famous Food: Freshwater fish dishes
  • Hotels: Chilika Resort, Nalaban Island Resort
  • Restaurants: Local lakeside eateries
  • Why Famous: Biodiversity hotspot and birdwatcher’s paradise

5. Udayagiri and Khandagiri Caves

Location: Near Bhubaneshwar

Famous For: Ancient Jain rock-cut caves

  • Attractions: Jain cave temples, intricate carvings
  • Best Time: October to February
  • Famous Food: Odia snacks from nearby Bhubaneshwar
  • Hotels: Hotels in Bhubaneshwar
  • Restaurants: Bhubaneshwar eateries
  • Why Famous: Historical Jain pilgrimage site with fine rock art

6. Cuttack

Location: Odisha

Famous For: Silver filigree work, Barabati Fort

  • Attractions: Barabati Fort, Mahanadi River, silver jewelry markets
  • Best Time: October to February
  • Famous Food: Rasgulla (original place), street food
  • Hotels: Hotel Swosti Premium, Mayfair Lagoon
  • Restaurants: Netaji Cake Shop, The Yellow Chilli
  • Why Famous: Cultural hub with rich heritage and handicrafts

7. Sambalpur

Location: Western Odisha

Famous For: Hirakud Dam, Sambalpuri sarees

  • Attractions: Hirakud Dam, Leaning Temple of Huma
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Famous Food: Local tribal cuisine, fish curry
  • Hotels: Hotel Pal Heights, Hotel Shakti
  • Restaurants: Local dhabas and restaurants
  • Why Famous: Known for handwoven sarees and scenic dam

8. Chandipur

Location: Balasore district

Famous For: Unique beach where the sea recedes up to 5 km twice a day

  • Attractions: Receding sea, beach walks
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Famous Food: Coastal seafood
  • Hotels: Limited hotels, nearby Balasore
  • Restaurants: Local eateries
  • Why Famous: Unique natural phenomenon of disappearing sea

9. Jeypore

Location: Koraput district

Famous For: Scenic hills and tribal culture

  • Attractions: Jagannath Temple, tribal markets
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Famous Food: Tribal dishes and local Odia cuisine
  • Hotels: Local guesthouses and resorts
  • Restaurants: Local eateries
  • Why Famous: Gateway to tribal Odisha with natural beauty

10. Dhauli

Location: Near Bhubaneshwar

Famous For: Site of the Kalinga War and Ashokan Peace Pagoda

  • Attractions: Peace Pagoda, Dhauli hill
  • Best Time: October to February
  • Famous Food: Odia traditional meals
  • Hotels: Hotels in Bhubaneshwar
  • Restaurants: Bhubaneshwar dining options
  • Why Famous: Historic Buddhist monument marking peace after war

11. Rayagada

Location: Southern Odisha

Famous For: Tribal culture and scenic surroundings

  • Attractions: Local temples, tribal handicrafts
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Famous Food: Tribal food specialties
  • Hotels: Local hotels and guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Local eateries
  • Why Famous: Rich tribal heritage and crafts

12. Sri Jagannath Puri Temple

Location: Puri

Famous For: Major Hindu pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Jagannath

  • Attractions: The temple complex, Rath Yatra festival
  • Best Time: October to February
  • Famous Food: Mahaprasad (holy food)
  • Hotels: Multiple accommodations from budget to luxury in Puri
  • Restaurants: Temple canteens and local eateries
  • Why Famous: One of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites and famous Rath Yatra

13. Lingaraja Temple

Location: Bhubaneshwar

Famous For: Iconic 11th-century Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva

  • Attractions: Temple architecture and religious ceremonies
  • Best Time: October to February
  • Famous Food: Odia vegetarian cuisine
  • Hotels: Hotels in Bhubaneshwar
  • Restaurants: Bhubaneshwar dining options
  • Why Famous: Largest temple in Bhubaneshwar with exquisite carvings

14. Raghurajpur Artist Village

Location: Near Puri

Famous For: Master craftsmen village famous for Pattachitra painting

  • Attractions: Workshops, traditional art and handicrafts
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Famous Food: Local Odia snacks
  • Hotels: Nearby hotels in Puri
  • Restaurants: Local eateries
  • Why Famous: Center for traditional Odia art and culture

15. Similipal National Park

Location: Mayurbhanj district

Famous For: Tiger reserve and biodiversity hotspot

  • Attractions: Wildlife safaris, waterfalls, flora and fauna
  • Best Time: November to March
  • Famous Food: Tribal cuisine
  • Hotels: Forest rest houses and resorts
  • Restaurants: Limited; mostly hotel dining
  • Why Famous: One of India’s prominent tiger reserves

Famous For: Tribal culture and scenic hills

  • Attractions: Tribal markets, natural beauty
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Famous Food: Tribal dishes
  • Hotels: Resorts and guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Local eateries

21. Daringbadi (Kashmir of Odisha)

Location: Kandhamal district

Famous For: Hill station with cool climate and scenic beauty

  • Attractions: Coffee plantations, waterfalls, valleys
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Famous Food: Tribal cuisine, local vegetables
  • Hotels: Local guesthouses and resorts
  • Restaurants: Small local eateries
  • Why Famous: Popular hill station known as the "Kashmir of Odisha"

Famous For: Ancient Buddhist monastery and archaeological site

  • Attractions: Stupas, monasteries, relics
  • Best Time: October to February
  • Famous Food: Odia vegetarian food nearby
  • Hotels: Hotels in Jajpur or Bhubaneshwar
  • Restaurants: Local eateries
  • Why Famous: One of the ancient centers of Buddhism in Odisha
