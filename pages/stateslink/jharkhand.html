Tourist Places in Jharkhand

1. Deoghar

Location: Santhal Pargana division, Jharkhand

Famous For: Baba Baidyanath Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas

  • Attractions: Baba Baidyanath Temple, Basukinath Temple, Trikuta Hills
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Food: Traditional Jharkhand cuisine, sweets like Khaja
  • Hotels: The Royal Residency, Hotel Sudha, local guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Hotel Nandini, local eateries around the temple

2. Jamshedpur

Location: East Singhbhum district

Famous For: Steel City of India, Tata Steel HQ

  • Attractions: Jubilee Park, Dimna Lake, Tata Steel Zoological Park
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Food: Street food, local Jharkhand dishes, North Indian cuisine
  • Hotels: Ginger Hotel, The Monarch, Hotel Centre Point
  • Restaurants: Jumboking, Hotel Maurya, local cafes

3. Ranchi

Location: Capital city of Jharkhand

Famous For: Waterfalls, parks, and tribal culture

  • Attractions: Tagore Hill, Rock Garden, Jonha Falls, Hanging Bridge
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Food: Litti Chokha, tribal cuisine, local snacks
  • Hotels: Radisson Blu, The Pinnacle, Hotel Capitol Hill
  • Restaurants: The Yellow Chilli, Openhouse, local dhabas

4. Betla National Park

Location: Latehar district

Famous For: Tiger reserve and rich wildlife

  • Attractions: Safari tours, waterfalls, Palamu Fort
  • Best Time: November to April
  • Food: Simple meals in forest lodges
  • Hotels: Betla National Park Jungle Lodge, forest rest houses
  • Restaurants: Lodge restaurants

5. Netarhat

Location: Latehar district

Famous For: Hill station known as “Queen of Chotanagpur”

  • Attractions: Sunrise and sunset points, waterfalls, Netarhat Dam
  • Best Time: October to February
  • Food: Local Jharkhand cuisine
  • Hotels: Netarhat Resort, guest houses
  • Restaurants: Limited, mostly at hotels

6. Hazaribagh

Location: Hazaribagh district

Famous For: Hazaribagh Wildlife Sanctuary, lakes, and hills

  • Attractions: Hazaribagh National Park, Canary Hill, Rajrappa Temple nearby
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Food: Traditional Jharkhand dishes
  • Hotels: Hotel Bodhi, local guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Local eateries and hotel restaurants

7. Bokaro

Location: Bokaro district

Famous For: Bokaro Steel Plant, modern city with parks

  • Attractions: City Park, Jawaharlal Nehru Biological Park
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Food: North Indian cuisine, street food
  • Hotels: Hotel Sheela, WelcomHeritage The Savera
  • Restaurants: Devidayal Bhojanalaya, local cafes

8. Palamu

Location: Palamu district

Famous For: Palamu Forts, wildlife, and natural beauty

  • Attractions: Palamu Fort, Betla National Park
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Food: Tribal cuisine, local flavors
  • Hotels: Basic lodges and guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Local eateries

9. Ghatshila

Location: East Singhbhum district

Famous For: Scenic beauty, waterfalls, and tribal culture

  • Attractions: Hundru Falls, Galudih Barrage
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Food: Traditional tribal cuisine
  • Hotels: Local guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Small local eateries

10. Shikharji (Parasnath Hill)

Location: Giridih district

Famous For: Important Jain pilgrimage site

  • Attractions: Temples on Parasnath Hill, pilgrimage routes
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Food: Simple vegetarian Jain food
  • Hotels: Guesthouses at base towns
  • Restaurants: Vegetarian eateries near pilgrimage spots

11. Dasham Falls

Location: Near Netarhat

Famous For: Scenic waterfall and nature spot

  • Attractions: Waterfall, hiking trails
  • Best Time: Monsoon and post-monsoon (July to November)
  • Food: Limited, pack food advised
  • Hotels: Nearby Netarhat hotels
  • Restaurants: None nearby

12. Jubilee Park, Jamshedpur

Location: Jamshedpur city

Famous For: Large urban park with lake and zoo

  • Attractions: Zoo, amusement rides, boating
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Food: Park food stalls
  • Hotels: Nearby city hotels
  • Restaurants: Near city restaurants

13. Baba Baidyanath Temple

Location: Deoghar

Famous For: One of the twelve Jyotirlingas, major Hindu pilgrimage

  • Attractions: Temple complex, festivals like Shravan Mela
  • Best Time: July to September (Shravan month), October to March for pleasant weather
  • Food: Prasad (holy food), local vegetarian dishes
  • Hotels: Hotel Sudha, The Royal Residency, various dharamshalas
  • Restaurants: Temple canteens, local eateries

14. Patratu Valley

Location: Ramgarh district

Famous For: Scenic valley with winding roads and nature beauty

  • Attractions: Valley views, waterfalls, trekking
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Food: Local Jharkhand cuisine
  • Hotels: Limited; stay in Ramgarh or nearby towns
  • Restaurants: Few local dhabas
