Tourist Places in Mizoram

1. Aizawl

Location: Capital city of Mizoram

Famous For: Hill city with scenic views and culture

  • Attractions: Bara Bazar, Solomon’s Temple, local markets, Mizoram State Museum
  • Best Time to Visit: October to April
  • Famous Food: Smoked meat, bamboo shoot dishes
  • Hotels & Stay: Several hotels and guesthouses in Aizawl
  • Restaurants: Local cuisine and cafes
  • Why Famous: Cultural and administrative center with hilltop views

2. Lunglei

Location: Southern Mizoram

Famous For: Scenic hills and natural beauty

  • Attractions: Durtlang Hills, Bridge of Rock, natural landscapes
  • Best Time to Visit: October to April
  • Famous Food: Tribal cuisines
  • Hotels & Stay: Local guesthouses and small hotels
  • Restaurants: Local eateries
  • Why Famous: Quiet town surrounded by hills

3. Champhai

Location: Eastern Mizoram, border with Myanmar

Famous For: Border town with rich tribal culture and agriculture

  • Attractions: Rice fields, border market, historical sites
  • Best Time to Visit: October to April
  • Famous Food: Local smoked meat, bamboo shoot curry
  • Hotels & Stay: Guesthouses and lodges
  • Restaurants: Local cuisine
  • Why Famous: Gateway to Myanmar and agricultural hub

4. Mamit

  • Hotels & Stay: Limited guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Local eateries
  • Why Famous: Quiet and less commercialized area
