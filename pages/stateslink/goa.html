Tourists places in Goa

Palolem Beach

Location: Canacona, South Goa

Famous For: Crescent-shaped beach with calm waters and vibrant nightlife

  • Attractions: Beach shacks, dolphin spotting tours, kayaking
  • Best Time to Visit: November to February
  • Famous Food: Fresh seafood and Goan curries
  • Hotels & Stay: Beach huts, resorts, and guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Beachfront cafes and local eateries
  • Why Famous: Relaxed vibe and scenic beach

Baga Beach

Location: North Goa, near Calangute

Famous For: Nightlife, water sports, and lively beach scene

  • Attractions: Parasailing, jet skiing, Tito’s nightclub
  • Best Time to Visit: November to March
  • Famous Food: Beach shacks with Goan and international cuisines
  • Hotels & Stay: Resorts, hotels, and guesthouses
  • Restaurants: Tito’s Street and various beach shacks
  • Why Famous: Vibrant nightlife and water activities
